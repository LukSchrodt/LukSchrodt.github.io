Brownies Get this Guide as a PDF
Ingredients:

  • 100g Butter

  • 2 Fresh Eggs

  • 150g Sugar

  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla sugar

  • 200g Chocolate

  • 200g Flour

  • 200g Walnut kernels

Step 1:

First add 200g butter to a bowl.
Then mix it until there are some small spikes.

Step 2:

Next add 2 fresh eggs.
Add 150g of sugar and mix it until the dough gets brighter.

Step 3:

Add one teaspoon vanilla Sugar.

Step 4:

The next step is to melt 200g of chocolate.
Put the chocolate in a bowl and put the bowl in hot water. Now wait until the chocolate is fully molten.

Step 5:

While waiting for the chocolate to melt add 200g flour to the dough.

Step 6:

Also while waiting we can crush or cut 200g of Walnut kernels.

Step 7:

Now add the liquid chocolate to the dough and mix it.

Step 8:

Add your crushed/cut walnut kernels and mix it again.

Step 9:

Not put the dough onto a baking sheet. Spread it till it's one cm high.

Step 10:

Preheat the oven to 180 °C. While waiting you can do your dishes and the clean up.

Step 11:

As soon as the oven is ready put the baking sheet into the middle of the oven. Than set a timer for ten minutes.

Step 12:

After ten minutes check if the inside of the brownie is done. If it is, cut it into the size your would like to have. Else bake it for an additional five minutes.

Step 13:

Enjoy it!
